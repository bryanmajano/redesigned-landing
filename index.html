When Our Technology Goes To Work

People Go To Sleep

We've removed the layers of sheet foam present in every mattress on the market and replaced it with hundreds of individual, fabric encased, foam cushions. Engaged with its own individual spring, each cushion moves freely – making the SleepOvation mattress the most responsive, comfortable, and hygienic mattress on the market today. It is a mattress based on brand new, cutting edge, and patented technology set to revolutionize the way you sleep.

Responsive

Traditional mattresses have layers of sheet foam that do not adequately distribute the weight of your body. This creates areas of high pressure that can lead to back pain, discomfort, and restlessness. The SleepOvation mattress is composed of foam cushions that are each engaged with their own individual pocket spring. It feels as if you have hundreds of tiny mattresses that support your entire body and respond to your every movement.

Comfortable

Traditional mattresses restrict air movement and make you feel hot while you sleep. The SleepOvation mattress allows air to flow freely through its cushion layer, providing an unprecedented level of comfort. With its hundreds of individual foam cushions, the SleepOvation mattress provides a new level of comfort and cradles every inch of your body.

Hygienic

Traditional mattresses have layers of foam that harbor dust mites, germs, and dead skin cells. The SleepOvation mattress has individually sealed foam cushion pockets that allow you to easily clean and vacuum the inside of the mattress. The removable cover can be easily washed and ensures an extra level of hygiene. This makes our mattress the cleanest on the market today.

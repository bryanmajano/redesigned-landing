What We've Done Is Revolutionary

We've removed the layers of sheet foam present in almost every mattress on the market and replaced it with hundreds of individual, fabric encased, foam cushions. Engaged with its own individual spring, each cushion moves freely and makes the SleepOvation mattress the most responsive, comfortable, and hygienic mattress on the market today. This patent-pending innovation is the most significant advancement to mattress technology in the last 50 years.

Sleep Better to Live Better!


Responsive

Traditional mattresses are made of sheet foam that do not adequately distribute the weight of your body. This creates areas of high pressure that can lead to back pain, discomfort, and restlessness. The SleepOvation mattress is composed of foam cushions that are each engaged with their own individual pocket spring. It feels as if you have hundreds of tiny mattresses that support your entire body and respond to your every movement.

Comfortable

Traditional mattresses restrict air movement and make you feel hot while you sleep. The SleepOvation mattress allows air to flow freely through its cushion layer, providing an unprecedented level of comfort. With its hundreds of individual foam cushions, the SleepOvation mattress provides a new level of comfort and cradles every inch of your body.

Hygienic

Traditional mattresses have layers of foam that harbor dust mites, germs, and dead skin cells. The SleepOvation mattress has individually sealed foam cushion pockets that allow you to easily clean and vacuum the inside of the mattress. The removable cover can be easily washed and ensures an extra level of hygiene. This makes our mattress the cleanest on the market today.
